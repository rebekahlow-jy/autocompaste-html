Welcome!

This is the Copy-Paste Experiment, where you will using the following techniques to copy and paste text.

  • AutoComPaste
  • Ctrl-C, Ctrl-V

You will be guided through the steps below to complete the experiment.

  1. Complete the Pre Questionnaire in the `Pre Questionnaire` tab.
  2. Read the Instructions in the `Instructions` tab. If you have any further questions, please feel free to ask your experimenter.
  3. Click on the `Experiment` tab to begin your experiment.
  4. Complete the Post Questionnaire in the `Post Questionnaire` tab.
  5. That's all! Thank you for your time and participation.

Before starting the experiment, please write your participant number ID given by your experimenter. Participant IDs should only consist of alphanumeric characters.
